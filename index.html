Tour with us

We would like to invite you on a journey of adventure through the Uganda and the East African Region, the best destination for memorable African safaris. We will arrange the vacation of your choice from the popular gorilla trekking tours in Uganda ,Rwanda and Congo to the legendary traditional safaris in East Africa’s finest national parks and game reserves. Venture to Uganda’s great lakes and rivers, the source of the Nile, unspoiled national parks, tropical rain forests and authentic cultural experiences.

At salvaje safaris , we arrange tour and safari packages in Uganda , and EastAfrica. The gorilla tracking experience in these three countries is a once in a lifetime activity.

"We offer customized product solutions that meet your and your clients’ needs. With experience and a culturally diverse team based in Uganda, we have a global understanding of tourism needs and expectations. With Eastern Africa being the heart of our operations, we as well know best how to implement these values on ground with our in-depth local expertise.

Experiences Guaranteed

Salvaje safaris aims to amaze every traveler; on any tour and every day. To us, this means that our travelers will feel alive while watching the sun set in the Masai Mara, standing barefeet in the red soil; shamelessly sip a glass of champagne at 7am, while enjoying an exclusive breakfast in the wilderness; still comprehending what you just experienced coming eye-to-eye with the Endangered Mountain Gorillas in Bwindi, diving into the picturesque Mutanda Lake just a few hours later. A feeling of intense satisfaction upon reaching the snow-capped peak of the Rwenzori Mountains; and the thrill upon spotting the members of the Big 5.

With our love for Africa, we promise unforgettable moments that can only be experienced on this mesmerizing continent, in the Salvaje safaris way; with care and respect for the destination and its people. Let us take you into our world!
