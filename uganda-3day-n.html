3 Days Source at the Nile and Sipi falls adventure

The Nile is the world’s longest river and its source is located in Jinja where it starts its long journey through Sudan and Egypt before pouring into the Mediterranean Sea. Take a boat trip to them spot where the river separates from the lake in the middle of the water.

The adventure continues to Sipi where you will hike to the 3 water falls and enjoy a coffee tour in the land of the Sebei as well as experience their rich culture. Wrap up the trip with an exciting game drive in the Pian Upe wildlife reserve in Karamoja.

Highlights

  • Visit to the source of the Nile
  • Sunset walk
  • Hiking the to Sipi water falls
  • Coffee tour
  • Game drive

Day 1: Nile Source and sunset walk

We pick you up from a place of your convenience to star the journey to Jinja which has come to be known as the Adventure harbour of Uganda. Enjoy a ride on the water to see the spot where Niver Nile originates.

Lunch in Jinja Town

Continue driving through several small towns to Sipi falls in Kapchorwa for check in.

Enjoy the sunset walk to a plateau where you will have an amazing view of the Karamoja flatlands below and the slopes of Mount Elgon and the waterfalls.

Dinner and overnight.

Day 2: Hiking and Coffee tour.

Enjoy hiking to the 3 magnificent water falls at the same time watching the Villagers go about their daily work in the coffee and banana plantations.

A coffee tour completes your day with the area known for production of Arabica coffee. You will learn about the coffee entire coffee production. From when a seed is planted to when its harvested, processed, roasted and finally brewed into a delicious cup of coffee.

Dinner and overnight.

Day 3: Game drive and return

We have a very early breakfast to embark on a short trip to descend to Pian Upe game reserve for a game drive.

This virgin wilderness is the second largest conservation area in Uganda and rich in game and bird life. Plain zebra, Impala, Common eland, giraffes are among the common sightings. Among the birds species, there’s the Ostrich being the unique an most sought after during game drive.

We drive back making a lunch stop over in Mbale

Drop off upon arrival at the previous pick up point.
