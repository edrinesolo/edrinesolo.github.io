Guides

Guides add a tremendous package to your safari.

The guides at salvage safaris have a high reputation for being experts in their field and being able to communicate this knowledge to those they travel with. They give learned information on a variety of different subjects, from flora and fauna to wildlife, bird life and cultural heritage.

Tour guides are responsible for your safety and carry emergency equipment, first aid kits, radios to communicate with the head office / camps and where appropriate either mobile or satellite phones. They will have detailed knowledge of the areas and will not only make what you see so much more understandable but are very interesting, enthusiastic, multi-talented, fascinating and jovial raconteurs.

When booking your tour with salvaje safaris, we assure your tour will be executed by a highly experienced professional tour guide. Multilingual guides with knowledge of alternative languages can be offered on request depending on the travel time and availability of our guides."

salvaje safaris is amongst the leading tour operators in Uganda who actively contributes towards developing the tourism industry in a way that reflects their care and respect for the destination and its people. Investing in quality guide training programs is one of the pillars we focus on when doing this. Our guide training programs range from intense adventure travel specialization courses for experienced tour guides, to extensive mentored training courses for young aspiring female tour guides.

All our guides have versatile knowledge about the destination, parks and activities,  culture and wildlife. May you require a guide with a specific specialty, such as a specialist birding guide or a guide with extensive knowledge on reptiles, always let us know so that we can make sure to make the right match for you.
